The Pitch

Pitch Narrative

Positioning

Archetype: Category Creation

Story Arc: Strategic Narrative (Raskin)

AI CFO is a new category sitting between accounting tools (Xero, QuickBooks) and fractional CFOs (GBP 1,000 to GBP 3,000 per month). Accounting tools record what happened. A fractional CFO tells you what it means. Ginger Bread does both, instantly, at a fraction of the cost.

Cold Email Template

Based on OpenVC best practice. Personalise the first two lines for every recipient.

Subject: Every founder becomes their own CFO. None of them are qualified. Hi [First name], [Why them: 1-2 sentences referencing their portfolio, thesis, or a specific investment. 15 minutes of research per email.] Ginger Bread is an AI CFO that gives B2B SaaS founders the financial clarity of a fractional CFO from day one. It connects to their bank, Xero, or QuickBooks and answers the questions that keep founders up at night. Key signals: - 3x founder (2x fintech), EUR 10M+ previously raised - Live prototype with UK bank APIs, Xero, and QuickBooks - 1,000+ founders in direct network across 20+ accelerator programmes - SEIS-eligible (50% income tax relief for investors) Raising GBP 250K pre-seed. 24 months runway. Default alive. Would you be open to a 30-minute call next week? Jamie Reynolds Founder, Ginger Bread

Email Rules

  • Personal greeting. Never "Dear Sir/Madam."
  • The "why them" lead is non-negotiable. 15 minutes of research per email. Reference their portfolio, thesis, a blog post, or a talk.
  • Value proposition in one sentence. No features, no roadmap.
  • Positive signals as bullets. Names and numbers, not "strong growth."
  • Clear CTA. "Would you be open to a 30-minute call next week?"
  • Attach the teaser deck (once built). Never attach the full presenting deck to a cold email.
