# GNV Fundraising Workshop

Build a demo-day-ready pitch with AI coaching.

## What this is

A fundraising and pitch training workshop by Ginger Ninja Ventures. Four exercises take you from raw evidence to a polished demo day pitch. Works with an AI coach (Claude Code, Cursor, ChatGPT) or with standalone templates.

## Two tracks

### Track 1: AI-Coached (recommended)

Clone the repo, open it in Claude Code (or Cursor/Copilot). The CLAUDE.md file coaches you through each exercise conversationally. Outputs are saved to `/outputs/`. See [SETUP.md](SETUP.md) for installation help.

### Track 2: Templates

Download the files from `/low-tech/`. Work through the structured templates in PowerPoint/Excel. No terminal needed.

## Exercises

1. **Pitch Narrative**. Evidence grid and storytelling. Build the foundation of your pitch from real traction data and a compelling narrative arc.

2. **Deck Building**. Slide-by-slide construction. Turn your narrative into a structured pitch deck, one slide at a time.

3. **Investor Readiness**. Self-assessment and regional context. Stress-test your pitch against what investors in your market actually look for.

4. **Demo Day Prep**. Practice, timing, delivery. Rehearse your pitch with feedback on pacing, clarity, and stage presence.

## Worked example

See `/examples/example-startup/` for a completed set of exercises showing what done looks like.

## Bring your own deck

Already have a pitch deck? Drop it into `/outputs/` and the AI coach will analyse it slide by slide.
